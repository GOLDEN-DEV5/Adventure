Skardu

📍 Skardu District, GB 🏷️ City / Adventure Hub 🕐 4–6 Days Ideal 📏 Altitude: 2,228m

About Skardu

Skardu is the adventure capital of Pakistan and the gateway to some of the world's highest mountains, including the mighty K2 — the second-highest peak on Earth at 8,611m. Situated in a broad valley at the junction of the Indus and Shigar rivers at an altitude of 2,228m, Skardu is a bustling city that serves as the base camp for mountaineering expeditions to K2, Broad Peak, and the Gasherbrums.

But Skardu is far more than just a mountaineering hub. The district is home to an extraordinary diversity of landscapes — from the cold Katpana Desert (the world's highest cold desert) to the lush green Shigar Valley, from the turquoise Satpara Lake to the dramatic Deosai Plateau. The ancient Kharpocho Fort, perched dramatically on a rocky crag above the city, is one of the most impressive historical monuments in the region.

The Skardu valley is also known for its warm and hospitable Balti people, their unique culture, cuisine, and centuries-old polyandric traditions. The region offers everything from world-class trekking to serene lake-side picnics, making it suitable for all types of travelers.

Top Attractions

  • 🏯
    Kharpocho Fort – A 16th-century fort perched on a 150m-high rock, offering sweeping views of the Skardu valley and Indus River far below.
  • 🏖️
    Katpana Cold Desert – One of the world's highest cold deserts, featuring dramatic sand dunes surrounded by snow-capped mountains. A unique and surreal landscape.
  • 🌊
    Satpara Lake – A beautiful natural lake 10 km south of Skardu, popular for trout fishing, boating, and picnics in a serene mountain setting.
  • 🏨
    Shangrila Resort (Lower Kachura Lake) – Often called "Heaven on Earth," this iconic resort sits beside a picture-perfect lake surrounded by fruit orchards.
  • 🏔️
    K2 Base Camp Trek – The ultimate adventure, starting from Skardu. A multi-week trek through the Baltoro Glacier to the base of K2 at 5,000m.
  • 🌿
    Deosai National Park – Accessible from Skardu, the world's second-highest plateau is a vast wildflower meadow and home to the rare Himalayan brown bear.

Travel Tips

✈️
Getting There

Daily flights from Islamabad (45 min). Book early — flights fill up fast and are weather-dependent.

🌤️
Best Season

June–September for trekking. May and October are great for sightseeing with fewer crowds.

🏨
Where to Stay

Shangrila Resort, Indus Hotel, and Mashabrum Hotel are top-rated options in Skardu city.

🍽️
Local Food

Try Balti cuisine — Mamtu (dumplings), Skyu (pasta stew), and fresh trout from Satpara Lake.
