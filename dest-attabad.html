Attabad Lake

📍 Gojal, Hunza 🏷️ Lake 🕐 1–2 Days Ideal 📏 Altitude: 2,638m

About Attabad Lake

Attabad Lake is one of the most dramatically beautiful and geologically fascinating lakes in all of Pakistan. Located in Gojal Valley in the upper Hunza region, the lake was formed on January 4, 2010, when a massive landslide near the village of Attabad blocked the flow of the Hunza River. The resulting flood submerged entire villages under water and created this stunning 21-km-long turquoise lake over the following months.

Despite its tragic origins, Attabad Lake has become one of Gilgit Baltistan's most iconic tourist attractions. The lake's extraordinarily vivid turquoise and blue-green color is caused by the glacial silt suspended in its waters, which interacts with sunlight to create a breathtaking spectacle. The lake is surrounded by towering, bare rocky mountains, creating a stark and dramatic contrast that makes for unforgettable photography.

Today, boat rides on Attabad Lake are one of the most popular tourist activities in the region. The KKH (Karakoram Highway) also passes through a series of five tunnels carved right through the mountains surrounding the lake — an engineering marvel in its own right.

Top Attractions & Activities

  • 🚤
    Boat Rides – Enjoy scenic boat rides across the turquoise lake, offered by local boatmen. The reflections of the mountains on the water are stunning.
  • 📸
    Photography Spots – Multiple viewpoints along the KKH offer world-class photography opportunities, especially at golden hour.
  • 🚇
    KKH Tunnels – Five remarkable tunnels were carved through the mountains after the landslide. Driving through them is a unique experience.
  • 🎣
    Fishing – The lake is stocked with trout and other fish. Local guides can arrange fishing trips.
  • 🏕️
    Camping – Camp on the lakeside under the stars — the silence and scenery at night are absolutely magical.

Travel Tips

🌤️
Best Season

May to October for calm weather. Boat rides are most enjoyable in June–August.

🚗
Getting There

30 km from Karimabad on KKH. Takes about 30–40 minutes by car via the tunnels.

💰
Boat Rides

Boat rides cost approximately PKR 500–1500 per person. Negotiate with local boatmen at the shore.

⚠️
Safety Note

Always wear a life jacket on the boat. Water is cold and currents can be strong near tunnel exits.
