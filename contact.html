Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we get asked most often.

What is the best time to visit Gilgit Baltistan?

The best time is April to October. Spring (April–May) brings cherry blossoms, while summer (June–August) is ideal for trekking and sightseeing.

Do I need a special permit to visit GB?

Pakistani nationals do not need a special permit. Foreign nationals may need a No Objection Certificate (NOC) for certain restricted areas such as near the Chinese border.

How do I get to Gilgit?

You can fly to Gilgit Airport from Islamabad (approx. 1 hour) or drive along the Karakoram Highway (12–15 hours). We can arrange airport pickups on request.

Can I request a custom itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in tailor-made tours. Contact us with your preferences and budget and we'll design a trip just for you.

What should I pack for the trip?

Layered clothing, sturdy trekking shoes, sunscreen, sunglasses, a first-aid kit, and a power bank are essentials. We'll send you a detailed packing list upon booking.

Is advance booking required?

We strongly recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (June–August) to ensure accommodation and transport availability.
