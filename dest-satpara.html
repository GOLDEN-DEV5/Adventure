Satpara Lake

📍 Skardu District, GB📏 Altitude: 2,636m (8,648 ft)🕐 Half Day – 1 Day Ideal

About Satpara Lake

Satpara Lake is a serene natural freshwater lake located just 10 kilometres south of Skardu city in Gilgit Baltistan. Sitting at an altitude of 2,636 metres, the lake is fed by the glaciers of the Satpara Glacier and is one of the primary drinking water sources for the entire Skardu district — a testament to the purity and clarity of its waters.

The lake's colour shifts between deep sapphire blue and brilliant turquoise depending on the time of day and weather conditions. Surrounding the lake are barren brown mountains that create a dramatic contrast with the vivid blue water. The reflections on still mornings are among the most beautiful natural scenes in Skardu.

Satpara Lake is known throughout Pakistan as one of the finest trout fishing destinations in the country. Brown and rainbow trout thrive in its cold, clean waters. The lake is also accessible by boat, and short boat rides are available for tourists who wish to experience the lake from the water. It is an ideal half-day excursion from Skardu and often combined with a visit to the nearby cold desert.

Top Attractions & Activities

  • 🎣
    Trout Fishing – One of the best trout fishing spots in Pakistan. Licences available locally. Fishing season is May–October.
  • 🚤
    Boat Rides – Short boat trips on the lake offer beautiful views of the surrounding mountains and pristine water. Very affordable.
  • 📸
    Photography – The still water in the morning reflects the surrounding mountains perfectly — arrive at sunrise for the best shots.
  • 🏖️
    Lakeside Picnic – A beautiful spot for a relaxed picnic. Local vendors sell snacks and fresh trout near the lakeshore.
  • 🥾
    Combined Skardu Day Trip – Combine Satpara with Kharpocho Fort and the Cold Desert for a full day of Skardu's highlights.

Travel Tips

🕐
Visit Time

Early morning offers the calmest water and best reflections. Midday is ideal for boating.

🚗
Getting There

10 km from Skardu city — a 20-minute drive. Taxis and rickshaws easily available from Skardu bazaar.

🎣
Fishing Permit

A fishing permit is required. Available from the Skardu Fisheries Department or through Golden Adventures.

🧥
Dress Warmly

The lake sits at altitude and morning temperatures can be cool even in summer. A light jacket is recommended.
